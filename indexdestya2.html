Chicken

Teriyaki Chicken has a simple sauce made of soy sauce, water, honey, vinegar, garlic and ginger. The perfect sticky sauce for serving over rice! yakiniku commonly refers to a style of cooking bite-size meat (usually beef and offal) and vegetables on gridirons or griddles over a flame of wood charcoals carbonized by dry distillation (sumibi, 炭火) or a gas/electric grill.
